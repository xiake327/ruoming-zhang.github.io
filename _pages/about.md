I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the [University of Delaware](https://www.udel.edu/), expected to graduate in August 2026.

My job market paper, published in the *Journal of Development Economics*, examines how China's 1999 higher education expansion propagated upstream to upper-secondary schooling and downstream to labor allocation, with differential effects by rural status.

**Research Fields:** Applied Microeconomics, Development Economics, Public Policy, Economics of Education, Demographic Economics
