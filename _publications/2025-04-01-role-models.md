**Under review at *Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization*** (with Zhejian Wang)

Using China's 2010 census and staggered emergence of provincial Gaokao top scorers, we find that exposure to a science-track top scorer increases schooling by 0.267 years and raises four-year college completion by 2.9 percentage points, highlighting the role of salient local role models in human-capital investment.
