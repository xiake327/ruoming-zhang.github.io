**Working Paper** (with Yiqun Tong, Zhejian Wang)

Using the 2015 1% Population Sample Survey and a cohort-based DiD/event-study design, we find that China's 1999 higher-education expansion made exposed rural cohorts more likely to end schooling at vocational high school, widening the rural–urban gap by about 3 percentage points.
