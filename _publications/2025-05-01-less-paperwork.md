**Working Paper** (with Zhejian Wang)

Using a monthly regression discontinuity in time design around China's October 2003 Marriage Registration Reform, we find that reducing documentary requirements raised first-marriage registration by about 3.7 per 1,000 unmarried urban residents per month, shifted match composition, and increased cross-jurisdiction marriages.
